## Add a Document

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your project dashboard">
    Click your project name from the sidebar or projects page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Document'">
    Find and select the 'New Document' button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    Enter a title, add your content, and click Save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify creation">
    Confirm your new document appears in the list.
  </Step>
</Steps>
