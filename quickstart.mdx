## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: GitHub account for version control integration

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to sign up and access your dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="globe">
    Navigate to `https://ilyaiinov.com/app` in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Click **Sign Up** and enter your email, name, and a secure password.
    
    Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the login page and authenticate with your credentials.
    
    You'll land on the main dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Project

Set up a new space for your project documentation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Project" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Documentation Space**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`, select a template (e.g., "Default"), and choose your brand color `#3B82F6`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="globe">
    Save and publish. Your space is now live at `https://ilyaiinov.com/docs/my-project`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
